About Me.

Who am I?

I am French and passionate about custom website development.Self-taught, I learned the concepts and tools of the web environment, such as HTML5, CCS3 and JavaScript.I am currently studying for a Master's degree in website and web application development, and I am expanding my knowledge in order to become more professional. In addition, I am doing internships in web agencies in order to enrich my experiences.

Learning Route.
